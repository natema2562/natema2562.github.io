The Artist Ryan Coyote

Offical Fan Page

An artist by the name of Ryan Coyote is taking the art world by storm with his thought provoking watercolor paintings. At first glance, a layman might find his artwork amateurish. But at a second glance you realize there is a lot more than that. And at a thrid glace you beging to fall in love with the clearly intentional mistakes and the appearence of being hastily completed for a school project.
With over 10 Million fans across the globe, Ryan Coyote is a seemingly unstopable force.



This webpage is dedicated to Ryan Coyote, who recently passed away due to complications with a bear-trap related injury. Our hearts go out to his ex-wives.
